VISION

“We live in a time when there are too many business people without jobs and too many jobs without people. This is primarily due to a global skills mismatch between what employers expect and what business professionals possess. With this in mind, EC-Council brainchild, the IIB Council, was created to train and certify business professionals on knowledge, technical and digital skills that are highly demanded in the job market.”
Jay Bavisi President and CEO, EC-Council

By utilizing its access to international resources such as trainers from India and elsewhere, IPSCOD has ensured training for various programmes meet global standards to render those who go through its programmes employable, not just in Kenya but anywhere in the world. The skill-based training programmes offered by IPSCOD and in partnership with IIHT as well as IIB are technology integrated, rendering the students multi-skilled and ready to work on day one of employment globally. By giving our young people that international status through relevant up-to-date job-ready skills training, IPSCOD is contributing not just to job creation but quality jobs creation. IPSCOD believes that in offering high quality IT skills training at its center at Westlands in Nairobi, Kenya can take advantage of its geographical position and well educated young population to provide skilled workforce to the rest of Africa as well as provide real time services to Europe with which it shares time zones, as well as the rest of the world.
